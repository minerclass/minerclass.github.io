The vocabulary is traveling.

In July 2026, EdSurge published a practitioner account of one school's response to generative AI and reached for unproductive success to name what it was seeing. Worth noting less as press than as evidence: a concept built for the qualifying paper turned out to be useful to educators describing their own classrooms.
