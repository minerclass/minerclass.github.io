Micah J. Miner, CETL, Ed.S.

Practitioner-scholar: district technology leadership and dissertation research on pedagogical friction.

I direct innovation and technology for Beach Park CCSD 3 and research what generative AI does to learning. My dissertation develops the Pedagogical Friction Framework: noetic, rhetorical, and existential friction name the productive struggle worth preserving, and infrastructural friction is the condition of possibility for the others. The projects below are public, interactive pieces of that work.
